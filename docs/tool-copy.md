## Launch Planner Intro

Set your launch date, stockpile, cadence, and promotion plans. Get a practical checklist from two weeks before launch through the first month after publishing.

## Launch Planner Warning Copy

Low stockpile:

Your current stockpile may not support this cadence for long. Consider reducing public release frequency or preparing more chapters before promising advance content.

Word count:

Many Royal Road launch discussions mention 10k-20k words as an important early threshold. This is not a guarantee, but launching below it may make momentum harder.

Rising Stars:

Rising Stars is algorithmic and changes over time. Treat this as a readiness checklist, not a ranking guarantee.

## Shoutout Tracker Intro

Keep swaps organized: who agreed, when it goes live, where the snippet goes, and whether it was confirmed.

## Shoutout Ethics Copy

Good swaps are relevant to readers and honest about fit. Avoid fake reviews, irrelevant recommendations, and spammy mass outreach.

## Patreon Calculator Intro

Estimate subscriber scenarios and check whether your advance-chapter promise is sustainable.

## Patreon Disclaimer

Revenue depends on story quality, audience trust, pricing, cadence, and reader demand. These scenarios are planning estimates, not income promises.

## Community Rules Intro

Before posting, check each community's current rules. Promotion, market research, AI, and surveys are treated very differently across Reddit, Royal Road forums, and Discord servers.
